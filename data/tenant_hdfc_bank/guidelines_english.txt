HDFC Bank — Customer Service Guidelines

LOAN AND EMI INFORMATION
Home Loan EMI: Your EMI depends on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.
EMI Due Date: EMI is auto-debited on the 5th of every month from your bank account.
EMI Default: Late payment beyond 3 days attracts ₹500 late payment penalty.
Prepayment: Allowed anytime. No charges on floating rate loans.
Foreclosure: Available after 6 months. Fixed rate loans: 2% foreclosure charge.
Loan Statement: Download via NetBanking or request at the branch.
Interest Certificate: Sent to your registered email after financial year end.

CUSTOMER SERVICE
Toll-Free Number: 1800-202-6161 (24x7, all networks)
Email Support: support@hdfcbank.com
Branch Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM
Saturday: 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM (select branches only)
NetBanking: Login at netbanking.hdfcbank.com
Mobile Banking: Download HDFC Bank MobileBanking app

ONLINE BANKING
NEFT / RTGS: Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 7:30 PM
UPI: Available 24x7

ACCOUNT SERVICES
Minimum Balance: Maintain ₹10,000 in savings accounts.
ATM Withdrawal Limit: Up to ₹1,00,000 per day.
Debit Card Block: Call 1800-202-6161 immediately or block via NetBanking.

KYC REQUIREMENTS
Accounts with incomplete KYC may be frozen.
Update KYC at your nearest branch or complete Video KYC online.
Required documents: PAN card, Aadhaar card, passport-sized photograph.

SAVINGS ACCOUNT RULES
Minimum Balance: ₹10,000 in metro cities, ₹5,000 in semi-urban areas.
Non-maintenance charge: ₹600 per quarter if minimum balance not maintained.
NEFT / RTGS transfers via NetBanking are free of charge.

EMERGENCY
Lost Card: Call 1800-202-6161 immediately.
Fraud Reporting: File a complaint at cybercrime.gov.in
Unauthorised Transaction: Notify bank within 24 hours for reversal eligibility.
