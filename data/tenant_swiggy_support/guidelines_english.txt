Swiggy — Customer Support Guidelines

ORDER TRACKING
Order Status: Track your order live in the "My Orders" section of the Swiggy app.
Delivery Time: Standard delivery time is 30-45 minutes after order placement.
Delayed Delivery: If delivery exceeds 60 minutes, track via app or contact support.
Order Not Moving: Refresh the app or contact customer care.
Live Tracking: View your delivery agent's real-time location on the in-app map.

REFUND POLICY
Wrong Item Received: Tap "I have an issue" in the app. Refund processed within 24 hours.
Order Not Received: Report immediately if the order was marked delivered without receipt.
Refund Timeline: Credit card: 5-7 business days. UPI / Swiggy Money: 24-48 hours.
Cash on Delivery: Cash refund not available. Credit issued as Swiggy Money.
Partial Order Missing: Report within 24 hours; missing item refunded to Swiggy Money.

FOOD QUALITY ISSUES
Stale or Incorrect Food: Report in app and upload photo as evidence.
Food Poisoning: Seek medical attention immediately. Call Swiggy at 1800-208-9999.

ORDER CANCELLATION
Cancel within 2 minutes of placing: Full refund issued.
Cancel after restaurant accepts: Cancellation fee may apply.
Restaurant-initiated cancellation: Full refund, no fee.

COMPLAINT ESCALATION
Level 1: In-app Help → "I have an issue with my order"
Level 2: Email support@swiggy.in — response within 24-48 hours.
Level 3: Call 080-6746-6789, available 8 AM to 11 PM daily.
Escalation: If unresolved in 72 hours, email supervisorcare@swiggy.in

SWIGGY ONE MEMBERSHIP
Benefits: Free delivery, exclusive discounts, priority support.
Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled within 7 days of purchase.
Auto-renewal: Notification sent 30 days before renewal date.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Helpline: 1800-208-9999 (24x7)
In-App Support: Swiggy app → Help → My Orders → Select relevant order
Email: support@swiggy.in

EMERGENCY
Delivery Agent Incident: Call 100 (Police) or 112 (Emergency Services).
Food Poisoning: Contact 1800-11-0100 (National Consumer Helpline).
